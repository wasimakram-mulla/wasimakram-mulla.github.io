Game Rules

Rules:

1
Movement Dice This dice is has numbers from 1 to 6, This die helps in movement of the player. The number on die after rolling will move the player that number of times.


Special Dice This dice is has six different images on it, This die helps in giving some special features to the player. The image on die after rolling will give different items to the player to help on different occations, below are details of items on a black die.

/Porter: These are your helpers which carries load and other tasks. You need atleast one porter in the game to continue playing.
/Food Crate: These are your food supplies for the journey. You need atleast 0.25 crate of food to survive in the game. Each porter in the game consumes 0.25 of crate per turn per player. When icon is faced up you will get 2 crates of food.
/Medicine Vial: These are medical supplies for diseases like "viral fewers" in the game, if at anytime you move over the viral fewer position you need atleast one vial of medicine to heal else you will lose a porter. When icon is faced up you will get 1 medicine vial.
/Gun: Gun and Bullets in the game are interlinked, There are ferocious animals like Wild Bear in the game which might kill you porter if you don't shoot it. You need 1 gun and 2 bullets to kill the animals. If anytime you get this die face up, you will get a gun which will lasts till the game.
/Bullet: As mentioned above you need 2 bullets to kill an animal. If anytime you get this die face up, you will get 1 bullet, which will be used once you go hunting.
/Katana: This is a sword which will help you to kill snakes in the game, killing a snake you will lose the sword. When icon is faced up you will get 1 Katana.

Game Tricks:

Start Again : Whenever you fall in this area, you need to Start the game again will the current resources.
Snakes : If you drop in this area Snakes will kill your porter, if you don't have any Katana sword. One Katana will kill one Snake.
Bear attack : If you drop in this area Bear will kill your porter, if you don't have any Gun and 2 Bullets. If you have Bullets, two of them will be used, Gun will remain as it is.
Viral Fewer : A fewer will spread across your camp, and you would require a Medicine Vial to cure this vial else one of you porter will get killed.
Town : These are the best townsmen you will ever meet, each time you get in a town, the townsmen will provide you with free food crate.
Jump : You will automatically jump over 2 places ahead.
Swamp : You will automatically thrown 4 places back.
